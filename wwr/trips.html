Our Top Rafting Trips

River Leven Rapids

Navigate the swirling eddies and fast-moving drops of the River Leven, a scenic route flanked by Lakeland beauty. Suitable for ages 10+ with moderate fitness.

Northampton Whitewater Centre

Perfect for first-timers or adrenaline junkies, this man-made course packs powerful rapids into a safe, controlled environment. Ideal for groups and team-building events.

Eden Valley Challenge

A longer guided excursion with Class III-IV rapids in a more remote setting. This immersive experience blends wild scenery with challenging white water. Suitable for ages 14+.
